What is Customer Churn?

    ->Customer churn = when a customer stops using a company’s product/service.

Examples

    ->Netflix user cancels subscription
    ->Telecom customer switches SIM
    ->Bank customer closes account


Business Question

    ->“Which customers are likely to leave soon?”

If a company knows this in advance, they can:

    ->Give discounts
    ->Call the customer
    ->Improve service
